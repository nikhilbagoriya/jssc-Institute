Director’s Message

Jay Shree Shyam Computer Institute

Welcome to Jay Shree Shyam Computer Institute, where we are committed to providing quality education in the field of digital technology and computer applications. In today’s fast-paced world, computer literacy is not just an advantage but a necessity. Our institute offers a range of courses, including RS-CIT, CCC, CCC+, Accounting, PGDCA, and more, designed to equip you with the skills required for a successful career in the IT and business sectors.

Our goal is to empower students with practical knowledge and hands-on experience, ensuring they stay ahead in this digital era. With experienced faculty, structured courses, and a student-centric approach, we strive to make learning both effective and enjoyable.

We invite you to embark on this journey of skill development and career enhancement with us. Together, let’s shape a brighter future!
